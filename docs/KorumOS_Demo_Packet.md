## What Is KorumOS?

KorumOS is a **decision intelligence platform** that convenes a council of five independent AI providers to analyze any question, cross-verify every claim, and produce structured, truth-scored intelligence briefs.

Instead of asking one AI and hoping it's right, KorumOS runs five experts **in sequence** — each one seeing what came before, each one building deeper — and then grades every claim for accuracy.

**The problem we solve:** Single-AI tools give you one perspective with no way to verify it. KorumOS gives you five independent analyses, surfaces where they agree and disagree, and scores every claim for truthfulness — the same multi-source corroboration principle used in real intelligence analysis.

## Core Capabilities

### 🎯 Truth Scoring
Every factual claim is cross-checked across all five providers:

| Score | Label | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 90–100 | **CONFIRMED** | Multiple AIs independently agree |
| 70–89 | **SUPPORTED** | Majority agreement |
| 50–69 | **UNVERIFIED** | Mixed signals — needs investigation |
| <50 | **CONTESTED** | AIs disagree — treat with caution |

### 🏷️ Intelligence Tagging
AI responses are automatically tagged with actionable markers:

| Tag | What It Means |
|-----|---------------|
| 🔴 **RISK VECTOR** | Identified threat or vulnerability |
| 🔵 **DECISION CANDIDATE** | Actionable recommendation |
| 🟣 **KEY METRIC** | Important number or statistic |
| 🟢 **VERIFIED FACT** | Cross-checked, high-confidence claim |

### ⚔️ Red Team Mode
After the council finishes, an additional AI runs with a "HACKER" persona to **attack the council's own conclusions** — finding blind spots, fatal flaws, and exploitable weaknesses. Essential for security and compliance assessments.

### ⚡ Sentinel (Quick-Strike)
Sub-2-second responses for follow-ups and quick lookups, with conversation memory for multi-turn dialogue.
