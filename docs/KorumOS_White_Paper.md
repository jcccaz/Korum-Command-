## Executive Summary

KorumOS is not a "Multi-AI Platform." It is an **Adversarial Decision Governance System** designed to produce high-fidelity, auditable intelligence in regulated and high-stakes environments. 

While others focus on generative volume, KorumOS focuses on **Decision Integrity**. The system achieves this by wrapping standard LLMs in a three-layer governance suit: the **Falcon Protocol** (PII/PHI Security), the **Confidence Governor** (Calibrated Truth-Scoring), and the **Audit Trail Ledger** (Immutable Provenance). The final product is a **Validated Intelligence Estimate (VIE)** — a hardened, cross-verified brief where every claim is truth-scored and source-mapped.

## 1. The Governance Stack (Architecture)

KorumOS separates the **Logic** from the **Intelligence**.

### Layer 1: The Falcon Protocol (Privacy Air-Gap)
Before any data reaches a model, the Falcon Layer redacts all PII/PHI. It uses a 3-pass identification engine and a deterministic **Mission Vault** to replace sensitive entities with "Ghost Maps." The models reason on the *relationships* between entities without ever seeing their raw identity.

### Layer 2: The Confidence Governor (Faith Calibration)
The Governor is the primary arbiter of truth. It enforces a **Dual-Confidence Model** that weighs factual density against decision risk. It applies mandatory **Scoring Floors** for verified evidence and **Hard Caps** for unresolved conflicts or source concentration risk. 

### Layer 3: The Audit Trail Ledger (ATL) (Legal-Grade Provenance)
Every event — from the initial Falcon hash to the final Vie composition — is recorded in an immutable, tamper-evident ledger. This allows auditors to reconstruct the exact "Forensic Trace" of how a decision was forged.

### Layer 4: The Neural Council (Modular Execution)
The Council is the interchangeable engine of the stack. It convenes five independent AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Perplexity, Mistral) to analyze queries sequentially. Each model builds on the work of the previous, providing a depth of reasoning that single-model systems cannot achieve.

## 2. The Validated Intelligence Estimate (VIE)

The VIE is the authoritative output of KorumOS. Unlike a "Response" or a "Summary," the VIE is a **Hardened Intelligence Brief**.

1.  **Truth Scoring**: Every claim is cross-verified across all five Council providers and assigned a status: `VERIFIED`, `LIKELY`, `UNKNOWN`, or `CONFLICTING`.
2.  **Adversarial Hardening**: A dedicated **Red Team** pass attacks the council's findings to find blind spots and exploitable flaws.
3.  **Diagnostic-First Gate**: If evidence fails to meet minimum verified thresholds, the system blocks "Premature Action" and forces a diagnostic investigation focus.
